Coverage / query slicing

When Google caps results (~300), slice the current query so each slice stays under the cap. Suggestions are built from what's already been discovered.

AI Triage

Feeds the captured results and the dork queries that produced them to an LLM, which investigates the corpus and reports the most important assets to investigate as prioritised findings. Authorised use only — your results and queries are sent to the configured provider.

Completed triage runs are saved here automatically.

Export

Exports apply your current filters — what you see is what you export (0 results).

Send to DB / webhook
